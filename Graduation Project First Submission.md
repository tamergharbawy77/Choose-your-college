##  Project Overview
[This application allows students to enter their total grades and search by total grade, geographic region, and type of study budget. From there, they can find information about available colleges, a brief overview of each college and its departments, career paths after graduation, job titles for each specialization, duration of study, expected salaries, and approximate expenses.]
